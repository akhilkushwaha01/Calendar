Lyndon Baines Johnson Day

Lyndon Baines Johnson's Birthday is an optional state holiday in Texas, that is always observed on August 27th. It commemorates the birthday of Lyndon Baines Johnson, the 36th President of the United States, on this day in 1908.

Independence Day

Indian Independence Day is always celebrated on August 15th. It is the National Day of India. Also known as 'I-Day', this public holiday marks the date in 1947, when India became an independent country. This holiday is a dry day in India, when the sale of alcohol is not permitted.
